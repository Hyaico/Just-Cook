Cookie Policy

The Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

Types of Cookies and Technologies We Use

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies remain on your Device when You go offline, while Session Cookies are deleted as soon as You close your web browser or exit the App.

We use both session and persistent Cookies and similar technologies for the purposes set out below:

  • Necessary / Essential Cookies

    Type: Session Cookies

    Administered by: Us

    Purpose: These Cookies are essential to provide You with services available through the Website and App and to enable You to use some of their features. They help to authenticate users, prevent fraudulent use of user accounts, and ensure the security of your data. Without these Cookies, the services that You have asked for cannot be provided, and We only use these Cookies to provide You with those services.

  • Functionality Cookies

    Type: Persistent Cookies

    Administered by: Us

    Purpose: These Cookies allow us to remember choices You make when You use the Website or App, such as remembering your preferences, device information, or language selection. The purpose of these Cookies is to provide You with a more personal experience and to avoid You having to re-enter your preferences every time You use the Website or App.

  • Analytics and Performance Cookies

    Type: Persistent Cookies

    Administered by: Us and Our Service Providers

    Purpose: These Cookies are used to collect information about how You use our Website and App, which pages or features You visit, the time spent on each page or feature, which links You click on, and other actions You take in the Website or App. This data is used to analyze and improve the performance and usability of our Website and App, and to understand user behaviors to enhance our services.

  • Social Media Authentication Cookies

    Type: Persistent Cookies

    Administered by: Third Parties (Google and Apple)

    Purpose: These Cookies are set by Google and Apple when You choose to log in to our App using their authentication services. They allow You to sign in to our App using your Google or Apple account credentials, eliminating the need to remember additional passwords and providing a more seamless user experience. These Cookies may collect certain information from your social media accounts as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

  • Tracking and Targeting Cookies

    Type: Persistent Cookies

    Administered by: Our Service Providers

    Purpose: These Cookies help us deliver relevant content and features based on Your interests and preferences. They may be used to deliver targeted advertising or to limit the number of times You see an advertisement. These Cookies remember that You have visited our Website or used our App and may share this information with other organizations such as advertisers.

Social Media Login Cookies

Our App offers You the ability to register and login using your Google or Apple accounts. When You choose to log in using these services:

  • Google may set cookies to authenticate Your identity and provide Us with access to basic profile information such as your email address, name, and profile picture.
  • Apple may use similar technologies to authenticate Your identity while providing enhanced privacy features, which may include options to hide your email address through their private email relay service.

The information collected through these authentication processes is subject to the respective privacy policies of Google and Apple, as well as Our own Privacy Policy. We use this information strictly for authentication purposes, to create and manage your account, and to provide our services to You.

By using social media login functionality, You authorize Us to collect, store, and use the information provided by these third-party services in accordance with this Cookies Policy and our Privacy Policy.

Mobile App Identifiers

In addition to cookies, our App may use device identifiers, mobile analytics software, and other similar technologies. These technologies collect information that helps Us analyze how users interact with our App, what features they use, and how to improve the user experience. These may include:

  • Device Identifiers: Unique identification numbers associated with your mobile device, such as the device ID or advertising ID.
  • Local Storage: Data stored locally on your device that allows the App to remember your preferences and settings.
  • Cache Data: Information stored temporarily to improve App performance and reduce loading times.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

If You prefer to avoid the use of Cookies on the Website, first You must disable the use of Cookies in your browser and then delete the Cookies saved in your browser associated with this website. You may use this option for preventing the use of Cookies at any time.

For the App, You can manage cookies and similar technologies through your device settings. Most mobile devices allow You to control tracking and analytics in the device settings or privacy menus. You can also reset your device's advertising identifier or opt-out of personalized ads.

If You do not accept Our Cookies or disable similar technologies in the App, You may experience some inconvenience in your use of the Website or App, and some features may not function properly.

If You'd like to delete Cookies from your browser or instruct your web browser to delete or refuse Cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser:

  • For the Chrome web browser, please visit this page from Google: https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/32050

  • For the Internet Explorer web browser, please visit this page from Microsoft: http://support.microsoft.com/kb/278835

  • For the Firefox web browser, please visit this page from Mozilla: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/delete-cookies-remove-info-websites-stored

  • For the Safari web browser, please visit this page from Apple: https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac

For any other web browser, please visit your web browser's official web pages.

Cookie Management on Mobile Devices

To manage cookies and data collection in our App:

  • For iOS devices: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy & Security" > "Tracking" and disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track". You can also manage app permissions in "Settings" > "Privacy & Security" > [specific permission categories].

  • For Android devices: Go to "Settings" > "Privacy" > "Ads" and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization". You can also manage app permissions in "Settings" > "Apps" > [select app] > "Permissions".

Do Not Track

Please note that We do not alter our Website's or App's data collection and use practices when We see a Do Not Track signal from your browser or device.

Data Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans. Most of our cookies are "session cookies," which means they are deleted when you end your session on our Website or close our App. Others are "persistent cookies," meaning they survive after your browser or app is closed. The expiration period for persistent cookies varies depending on the purpose of the cookie and the specific cookie provider.
